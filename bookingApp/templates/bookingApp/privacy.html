Privacy Policy

How GetMeBooked handles and protects your data

Effective Date

31 December 2025

01. Our Privacy Approach

GetMeBooked operates on a data-minimal principle. We collect only the information necessary to operate a secure booking and scheduling platform.

This Privacy Policy complies with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

02. Information We Collect

The type of data collected depends on whether you are a Client or a Business Owner.

  • Client Information: Name, email address, phone number, and booking history.
  • Business Owner Information: Business name, contact details, operating hours, services, and staff availability.
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and security-related metadata.
03. Payment & Financial Data

GetMeBooked does not store credit card details, bank account numbers, or full payment credentials.

All subscription payments and booking deposits are processed via PayFast, a third-party payment provider.

  • Deposit payments flow directly from the Client to the Business Owner.
  • We store only payment status and subscription expiry dates to manage platform access.
04. Location & Address Data

GetMeBooked does not track your real-time location or device GPS data.

Business Owners may provide a physical business address, which is displayed publicly on their booking page for client convenience.

05. Cookies & Tracking

We use only essential cookies required for authentication and platform functionality.

  • No advertising cookies
  • No tracking pixels
  • No behavioural profiling
06. Data Sharing

Your data is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information is shared only where required to:

  • Notify businesses of appointment requests
  • Send booking confirmations and reminders
  • Process payments through PayFast
07. Data Security

Sensitive information such as PayFast merchant credentials is stored using industry-standard encryption.

We implement reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

08. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

09. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data.

Account deletion will remove personal identifiers from active systems, subject to legal and operational retention requirements.

Privacy Commitment

By using GetMeBooked, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.
