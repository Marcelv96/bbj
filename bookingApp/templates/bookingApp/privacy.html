Privacy Policy

Our commitment to data minimalism for getmebooked.co.za

Effective Revision

31 December 2025

01. Data Collection

We operate on a strictly "minimal-essential" data policy. We only collect information required to facilitate scheduling and verify identity.

User Account Data: We store your Email, Username, and Phone Number to manage authentication, appointment notifications, and booking history.

Merchant Credentials: For Business Owners, we store PayFast Merchant IDs and security keys. These are encrypted and used solely to route client deposits to your business.

02. Business Address

We do not track or store the physical location of users.

The only address information stored on our platform is the **Physical Business Address** provided by the Business Owner. This address is used exclusively to display an interactive map via iframe on the business's public booking page to help clients find the premises.

03. Financial Processing

We do not store credit card details or bank account numbers.

All transactions—including platform subscriptions and booking deposits—are processed through **PayFast**, a secure third-party provider. We only store transaction status and subscription expiration dates to manage access to the management portal.

04. Usage Disclosure

Your data is never for sale.

Information is used exclusively to notify Business Owners of your Appointment requests and to automate the confirmation of paid deposits. We do not use tracking pixels, third-party marketing cookies, or behavioral analytics.

05. Your Rights

You have the right to delete your account at any time. Deleting your account will immediately purge your Profile, encrypted Merchant Keys, and Appointment history from our active database. Public reviews will be anonymized upon request.

Privacy First

By using GetMeBooked, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.
