01. Service Scope

GetMeBooked is a scheduling infrastructure provider. We facilitate the connection between independent Business Owners (Salons, Barbers, and Clinics) and their clients.

We are not a service provider, employer, or agent for any business listed on the platform. Any contract for physical services is strictly between the User and the Business Owner. We do not guarantee the quality, safety, or legality of services rendered at physical locations.

02. Financial Policy

Subscription & Deposit Infrastructure

02.1 Platform Subscriptions

Access to GetMeBooked management tools is provided on a month-to-month basis. Business Owners manage renewals manually via their dashboard.

  • Additive Renewal: When a renewal payment is processed, 30 days are added to your current expiration date. Renewing early does not result in lost days.
  • Alerts & Access: A payment link is always available. Alerts will appear as your subscription nears its end.
  • Grace Period: Failure to renew by the expiration date will result in the immediate suspension of your public booking page.
02.2 Merchant Deposits

Business Owners may require deposits via their own PayFast Merchant account. GetMeBooked provides the integration but does not touch these funds.

  • Direct Flow: Payments go directly from Client to Business Owner.
  • Disputes: All refund requests for booking deposits must be settled directly with the Business Owner.
03. Booking Lifecycle

The confirmation process depends on the business's deposit settings.

Deposit-Required Bookings

Upon requesting a slot, clients receive an automated PayFast payment link via email. The appointment is automatically confirmed only once payment is successful. Requests that remain unpaid for more than 2 hours are automatically cancelled by the system to release the time slot.

Standard Bookings

For services not requiring a deposit, appointments enter a "Pending" state. Business Owners must manually confirm or decline these requests through the Management Portal. A pending request is not a guarantee of service.

Cancellations: Users are subject to the specific business's local no-show and late-cancellation policies once a booking is confirmed.

04. Data Security

We prioritize the integrity of your business data and financial credentials.

Encryption Standard: PayFast Merchant IDs and security keys are stored using industry-standard encryption. We never store raw credit card details or client payment information on our local servers. Data is used strictly to facilitate the booking and subscription renewal process.

05. Content Integrity

Users may leave one review per business entity. Reviews must be honest and based on first-hand service experiences. We reserve the right to remove content that is defamatory, fraudulent, or violates community standards.
