Client Directory Guide

LTV Revenue

Lifetime Value. This tracks the total confirmed revenue generated by a client since they joined. Use the "Sort by Revenue" filter to identify your VIP spenders.

Engagement Score

Tracks total completed bookings versus their join date. High booking counts in a short time indicate a loyal, "Power User" client.

Retention Alert

A client is marked Overdue if they haven't visited in 30 days. Use the WhatsApp button to send a quick re-engagement message.

Deposit Management

You can override global business settings for specific trusted clients using the toggle switch in the client list or modal.

Deposit Required

Default. Client must pay online to confirm.

Exempt (No Deposit)

Trusted VIPs. Can book without immediate payment.
